## 1. Clean the teflon tube

If both of these methods didn't work you might also want to check if there are any impurities in the teflon tube. **First, remove the nozzle and then pull filament trough the teflon tube a couple of times.**

## 2. Clean the hotend pipe

The last option is to also clean the hotend pipe. For this you need to **remove the teflon tube and pull it trough the hotend pipe a couple times**.
